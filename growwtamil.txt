How To Trade On Expiry Day in Tamil

Check the Previous Day's high and Low


Wait the first 15 minutes
Then check for 15 minutes high and low using indicator ORB
Buy ITM put option when 15 mins low broken
Stop loss for put option trade, 1st 15 mins high on the spot

Buy ITM Call Option When 15 mins High Broken
Stop loss for call option trade, 1st 15 mins low on spot

Candle Patterns Alert
Use indicator -Candle Patterns Alert
Engulfing Pattern
if goes above engulfing then buy the call option
if goes below engulfing then by put option

Reversal candle pattern setup
